Responsive design

Grid and Flex

For this assignment I wanted to use flex and grid for the layout. I tried making the grids responsive as possible without putting it in media queries, but some grids I put in a mediaquery cause I needed it to have two collumns on desktop but on mobile on top of eachother. For the UI-cards I used flex-wrap. I used an outer boxshadow instead of a border to give the visual of the cards floating.
